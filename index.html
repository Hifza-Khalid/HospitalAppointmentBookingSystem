Abstract

The Hospital Appointment Booking System is developed to streamline the process of booking, updating, viewing, and canceling appointments in hospitals. It addresses the problems of long queues and mismanagement by allowing patients to book appointments remotely and enabling hospital staff to manage schedules efficiently. The system ensures data accuracy, reduces patient waiting time, and improves overall healthcare service quality.

1.2 Operations on the Hospital Appointment Book

1.2.1 Operation: AddAppointment

Adds a new appointment if the patient is not already scheduled.

1.2.2 Operation: FindAppointment

Finds the appointment time for an existing patient without changing the system state.

1.2.3 Operation: UpdateAppointment

Updates the appointment slot for an existing patient.

1.2.4 Operation: ViewAppointments

Views the appointment slot for a specific patient.

1.2.5 Operation: CancelAppointment

Cancels a patient‚Äôs appointment and removes them from the system.
